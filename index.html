Who We Are

A Registered NGO Built on
Transparency & Purpose

"Uniting minds for a change"
Section 8 80G & 12A CSR-1 NITI Aayog ISO 9001:2015 NGO Darpan

Founded on 23 September 2020 by Mr. Govind Shukla, InAmigos Foundation is a Section 8 non-profit organisation headquartered in Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh — driven by the belief that change begins with unity.

With ISO 9001:2015 accreditation, 80G & 12A tax certifications, CSR-1 approval, and NITI Aayog registration, InAmigos maintains full organisational transparency — ensuring every rupee donated creates real, measurable impact.

From feeding drives and classroom education to animal rescue missions and women's skill workshops, InAmigos mobilises volunteers, interns, and corporate partners to serve underserved communities across 28 states.

🍽️ Feeding Hope

Through Project SEVA's food distribution drives, InAmigos ensures no one sleeps on an empty stomach. Volunteers serve meals on Independence Day, festivals, and every ordinary day that someone needs nourishment.

📖 Education for All

Bachpanshala creates safe learning spaces for children denied access to formal schooling due to poverty. Books, guidance, and encouragement plant seeds of a transformed future.

💪 Women Rising

Project Udaan has helped women across multiple states start micro-businesses, learn digital tools, and build financial independence — transforming dependence into dignity.

🌱 A Greener India

Prakriti and Jeev together remind us sustainability is about respecting every living being. From plantation drives to animal rescue, InAmigos walks this ethos every single day.
